## The problem vitalsync solves
Most productivity and wellness apps fails for three reasons;
1. No accountability
Users self report habit with no structure or verification making the progress hard to trust
2. Poor long term motivation
Streaks break, enthusiasm fades and there is no meaningful incentive to stay consistent
3. Little actionable feedback
User track datat but receive no insight into why they are improving or declining 
Vitalsync adresses these gaps by using blockchin for transparency, smart contracts for fair rewards, and AI for personalised insight generation
